Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Business Tracker Premium ("the Add-on") is a Google Workspace Add-on built using Google Apps Script. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Access

The Add-on accesses the following data within your Google Sheets spreadsheet:

  • Spreadsheet data you enter (orders, clients, invoices, payments, expenses)
  • Your Google account email address (for license verification purposes)

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not transmit your spreadsheet data to any external server
  • We do not store your business data outside of your own Google Sheet
  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

3. License Verification

When you activate your license key, the Add-on contacts the Gumroad API solely to verify your license key is valid. Only your license key and email address are transmitted for this purpose. No spreadsheet data is sent.

4. Script Properties

The Add-on stores your business configuration (business name, address, invoice prefix, tax rate) in Google Apps Script Properties. This data is stored within Google's infrastructure and is only accessible within your script instance.

5. Google API Scopes

The Add-on uses the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • spreadsheets — to read and write data in your Google Sheet
  • script.container.ui — to display dialog boxes
  • script.storage — to store your settings in Script Properties
  • userinfo.email — to identify your account for license verification

These scopes are used exclusively for the functionality described above, in accordance with Google's Limited Use requirements.

6. Data Retention

All your data resides in your own Google Sheet. Uninstalling the Add-on does not delete your spreadsheet data. Script Properties can be cleared by the user at any time.
