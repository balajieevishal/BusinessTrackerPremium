Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my license key?
After installing the Add-on, go to the 📊 Business Tracker menu → 🔑 Activate License. Enter the license key you received from Gumroad and click Activate.
The menu is not showing after installation. What do I do?
Refresh your Google Sheet (Ctrl+R / Cmd+R). If the menu still doesn't appear, go to Extensions → Apps Script → Run → onOpen.
Can I use the same license key on multiple Google accounts?
Each license key is for a single Google account. To use on multiple accounts, please purchase additional licenses.
My invoice numbers are wrong. How do I fix them?
Go to ⚙️ Business Settings and verify your Invoice Prefix and Financial Year Start Month are correctly configured.
Is my data safe?
Yes. All your business data stays in your own Google Sheet. We never access or store your spreadsheet data on external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Does it work for businesses outside India?
Yes. You can configure your own currency symbol, tax name (GST/VAT/Sales Tax), tax rate, and financial year start month during setup.
